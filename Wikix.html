February Tuesday 2021

Robert Glasper


Robert is a famous musician, producer, and arranger. He is an incredible pianist/keyboardist and one of my many insperations behind my playing.
Black Radio 2 still stands out to be one of my favorite albums by him and featuring a plethora of talented artists on top of his crazy arrangements.


  • He has won multiple grammies

  • He is included in various big projects around the industry, even with his numerous music groups he performs and record with.

  • A very motivational individual and loves what he does.



Chris Coleman


Chris is a powerhouse musician/producer who does multiple clinics around the world to help the drummer community in a major way.
Chris as a musician is incredible! Meeting him in person for my birthday one year before playing a show? Even better!
He is an all around great guy and very down to earth, also quite fun to be around he's very funny.

  • Powerhouse drummer and an amazing bass player

  • Amazing person and always motivational no matter who he meets

  • This man has seen the world playing drums and bass



Ryan Leslie


Ryan has to be one of my all time favorite singer/songwriter/musician/producer.
This man has produced for so many people in the industry and he managed to be able to do so after overcoming homelessnes.
He has been a powerful insperation to my music creation process in the studio and makes me want to continue my journey as a producer.


  • Came into success after being able to show his talents while being homeless

  • Incredibly business minded after he had to sacrifice his entire music catalog due to a jerk who found his missing laptop

  • Can play any instrument just enough to get the right song for the track that he is creating
